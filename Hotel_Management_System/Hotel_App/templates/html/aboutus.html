R Hotels is an international hotel chain headquartered in the United States. A division of the R Hotel Group, it operates the brands R Blu, R Red, R Collection, Country Inn & Suites, and Park Inn by R among others.

The majority of R-branded hotels are located in the United States. The company's headquarters, as well as the headquarters of the owner, R Hospitality, Inc., are located in Minnetonka, Minnesota, a suburb of Minneapolis, the city where the first R Hotel was built. The original R Hotel, opened in 1909, was at 41 South Seventh Street in Minneapolis.
